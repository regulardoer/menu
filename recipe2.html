Veggie Buddha Bowl

⏱️ Prep Time: 15 mins 🍳 Cook Time: 25 mins Difficulty: Easy

📝 Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 sweet potato, cubed
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained
  • 4 cups spinach or kale
  • Tahini dressing: 3 tbsp tahini, lemon juice, garlic, water
  • Avocado for topping

👩‍🍳 Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Cook quinoa in vegetable broth according to package instructions.
  2. Preheat oven to 400°F. Toss sweet potato, zucchini, bell pepper with oil and salt.
  3. Roast vegetables for 20-25 minutes until tender.
  4. While roasting, make dressing: whisk tahini, lemon juice, minced garlic, and water until smooth.
  5. Divide quinoa into bowls. Arrange roasted veggies, chickpeas, and fresh greens.
  6. Top with avocado slices and drizzle generously with tahini dressing.
  7. Sprinkle sesame seeds if desired. Enjoy!
