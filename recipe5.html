Berry Chia Pudding

⏱️ Prep Time: 5 mins ⏰ Chill Time: 4 hours (or overnight) Difficulty: Easy

📝 Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup chia seeds
  • 1 cup coconut milk (or any plant milk)
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup or honey
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup mixed berries (fresh or frozen)
  • Granola for topping
  • Toasted coconut flakes
  • Fresh mint leaves

👩‍🍳 Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. In a jar or bowl, whisk together chia seeds, coconut milk, maple syrup, and vanilla.
  2. Let sit for 5 minutes, then whisk again to prevent clumps.
  3. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight until thickened.
  4. Layer pudding with berries in serving glasses.
  5. Top with granola, coconut flakes, and fresh mint.
  6. Serve chilled. Can be stored in fridge for up to 5 days.
