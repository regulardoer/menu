Spicy Tofu Tacos

⏱️ Prep Time: 15 mins 🍳 Cook Time: 15 mins Difficulty: Medium

📝 Ingredients

  • 1 block extra-firm tofu, pressed and crumbled
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • Corn tortillas (8-10)
  • For slaw: 2 cups red cabbage, lime juice, cilantro
  • Avocado crema: 1 avocado, lime, cilantro, salt
  • Pickled red onions and hot sauce

👩‍🍳 Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Press tofu for 15 minutes to remove excess water. Crumble into small pieces.
  2. Heat oil in skillet. Add crumbled tofu and spices. Cook 8-10 minutes until crispy.
  3. Make slaw: toss cabbage with lime juice, salt, and chopped cilantro.
  4. Blend avocado, lime juice, cilantro, and salt until smooth for crema.
  5. Warm corn tortillas on skillet or directly over flame.
  6. Assemble tacos: spicy tofu, slaw, drizzle of avocado crema.
  7. Top with pickled onions and hot sauce. Serve with lime wedges.
