Creamy Mushroom Pasta

⏱️ Prep Time: 10 mins 🍳 Cook Time: 20 mins Difficulty: Medium

📝 Ingredients

  • 12 oz fettuccine or pappardelle pasta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 tbsp butter, divided
  • 1 lb mixed mushrooms (cremini, shiitake, oyster)
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh parsley and thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

👩‍🍳 Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Cook pasta in salted water until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water.
  2. Heat oil and 2 tbsp butter in large skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Add mushrooms and cook until golden brown (8-10 minutes).
  4. Add garlic and thyme, cook 1 minute until fragrant.
  5. Pour in heavy cream, bring to simmer. Add Parmesan cheese.
  6. Toss cooked pasta into sauce, adding reserved pasta water to adjust consistency.
  7. Stir in remaining butter for extra richness.
  8. Garnish with fresh parsley and extra Parmesan. Serve immediately.
