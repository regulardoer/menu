Classic Margherita Pizza

⏱️ Prep Time: 20 mins 🍳 Cook Time: 12 mins Difficulty: Easy

📝 Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce (San Marzano preferred)
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves (about 12-15)
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Sea salt and black pepper to taste
  • Optional: red pepper flakes

👩‍🍳 Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C) with pizza stone if available.
  2. Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface to 12-inch circle.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly, leaving 1/2-inch border.
  4. Arrange mozzarella slices over sauce. Drizzle with olive oil.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes until crust is golden and cheese bubbles.
  6. Remove from oven, top with fresh basil leaves immediately.
  7. Season with salt, pepper, and optional red pepper flakes.
  8. Slice and serve hot!
